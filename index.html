School Shooting in USA From 1990 - 2020

Today we gonna explore this question: "Is school shooting a problem?"

This is the first section

This choropleth map is the representation of North America, showing the percentage of the victims that being involed in school shooting incidents from each State, being calculated through the number of enrolments.

The number of victims are being calculated from the total of wounded, and fatalities cases. The map contains a few functionalities such as a percentage victims range slider, and a selection of state or states.

Note: You can select the state by clicking on the map, and hold SHIFT to select multiple states, and click anywhere that's not a state to cancel.

This is the second section

Similar to the previous section, this section will mainly focusing on showing the average number of victims from 1990 - 2020, comparing between each type of schools.

This visualization provides a legend selection, and a drop down option selection.

Through the making of this visualization, surprisingly elementary school was consisting of a really high average around the year of 2012 and 2018

Note: you can interact with the legend by clicking on the type of school you would love to see, hold SHIFT to select multiple, click anywhere outside of the visualization to cancel.

This is the third section

This section will be a supporting tool to the previous section, in which it's showing an overview average number of victims from 1990 - 2020

This section will contains a interation in which you can pick which interval of years to look at.

Note: The selection of interval can be used by clicking and dragging your mouse over the visualization. The MOUSE-UP and MOUSE-DOWN can be used to change the size of interval, as well as moving the interval around by dragging it, and click anywhere inside of the visualization that's not being covered by the interval to cancel.

This is the Fourth section

This section will be a supporting tool to the map representation of North America, in which this will be a comparision between each state of United State through the their area types.

The comparision will consisting of Urban, Suburban, Rural, and N/A(which can be consider as unknown)
